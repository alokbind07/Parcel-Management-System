Streamline Your Shipping Experience

Our web-based Parcel Management System offers a comprehensive solution for customers and delivery officers to manage shipping needs efficiently. From booking to delivery tracking, we provide a seamless end-to-end service.

Key Features

  • Easy User Registration: Quick and secure account creation
  • Personalized Dashboards: Role-specific interfaces for customers and officers
  • Simple Booking Process: Capture complete parcel and recipient details
  • Flexible Delivery Options: Choose pickup and drop-off times
  • Generate Invoicing: Generate detailed invoices
  • Booking History: Access past records

User Roles

  • Customers: Book services, track parcels, view booking history,generate invoice.
  • Delivery Officers: Manage pickups, update delivery status, schedule deliveries,book parcel
